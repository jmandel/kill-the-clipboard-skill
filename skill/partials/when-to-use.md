## When to Use This Skill

Use it when the patient wants to:

- Share their health records with a doctor, clinic, hospital, or other care setting
- Prepare for an upcoming visit ("my appointment is Thursday — can you get my records ready?")
- Create a SMART Health Link or a QR code for their health data
- Skip or speed up paper check-in forms ("kill the clipboard")
- Bring a new provider up to speed with whatever parts of their history matter
- Tell their side of the story alongside the data (the Patient Story PDF)
- Manage a link they already created: check who accessed it, extend it after it
  expired (re-arm), pause it, change its label, or destroy it

**Out of scope (phase 1):**

- **Getting the records in the first place.** This skill shares records the patient
  already has on disk. If they have none, recommend
  [health-skillz](https://health-skillz.joshuamandel.com) — it connects to patient
  portals via SMART on FHIR and downloads their data locally — then come back here.
- **Insurance card capture** (photo → CARIN digital insurance card) — planned, not yet.
- **Arbitrary free-form content as data.** The share is structured FHIR plus the two
  PDF kinds. The Patient Story PDF is the supported home for the patient's own words.
